Support

Frequently asked questions and ways to reach us.

FAQ

What devices are supported?

LaserBeam requires iOS 16.0 or later and supports iPhone and iPad with A12 chip or newer (devices with Metal support). The game is optimized for both iPhone and iPad in landscape orientation.

How do I remove ads?

You can remove all ads permanently through a one-time in-app purchase. Tap the "Remove Ads" button in the game's settings menu to purchase. This is a lifetime purchase — no subscription required.

The game crashes on launch — what should I do?

Try these steps: (1) Force close the app and reopen. (2) Restart your device. (3) Ensure you have the latest iOS version. (4) If the issue persists, contact us with your device model and iOS version.

Is my progress saved?

Yes. Your progress is saved locally on your device and synced to your account if you sign in. If you reinstall the game, sign in with the same account to restore progress.

How do I report a bug?

Email us at raadbadr@gmail.com with a description of the bug, your device model, iOS version, and steps to reproduce. Screenshots or screen recordings are very helpful.

Will there be new levels?

Yes! We're actively developing new levels and content for LaserBeam. Follow us on X for updates on new releases.
