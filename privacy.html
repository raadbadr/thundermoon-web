1. Overview

ThunderMoon ("we", "us", or "our") operates mobile games including LaserBeam ("the Game"). This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your personal information.

By downloading and playing the Game, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

2. Data We Collect

We collect the following types of data:

  • Account data: Email address and authentication tokens when you create an account or sign in.
  • Game progress: Level completion, scores, and gameplay statistics.
  • Device data: Device model, iOS version, and crash reports for debugging.
  • Ad interaction data: Impression and click data for served ads (via Google AdMob).
  • Identifier data: IDFA (Identifier for Advertisers) — only if you grant permission via Apple's App Tracking Transparency (ATT) prompt.

3. Advertising (Google AdMob)

The Game uses Google Mobile Ads (AdMob) to display advertisements. Google may collect and use data to serve ads, including:

  • Device identifier (IDFA) — only with your consent
  • Approximate location
  • Device information (model, OS version, language)
  • Ad interaction data (views, clicks, conversions)

Google's use of advertising data is governed by Google's Privacy Policy. We do not control how Google uses this data.

You can remove all ads by purchasing the "Remove Ads" in-app purchase.

4. Tracking & App Tracking Transparency (ATT)

In accordance with Apple's App Tracking Transparency framework:

  • We request your permission before tracking your activity across other companies' apps and websites.
  • If you deny tracking permission, the Game will still function normally — ads will be served contextually without personalized tracking.
  • You can change your tracking preference at any time in iOS Settings → Privacy & Security → Tracking.

5. Authentication (Supabase)

The Game uses Supabase for user authentication and cloud data storage. When you sign in:

  • Your email and auth tokens are transmitted securely to Supabase.
  • Game progress is synced to your account on Supabase servers (European Union).
  • We use Supabase's Row Level Security (RLS) policies to ensure users can only access their own data.
  • See Supabase's Privacy Policy for details.

6. In-App Purchases

The Game offers a one-time "Remove Ads" purchase. Purchase processing is handled by Apple's StoreKit. We receive a record of your purchase to disable ads — we do not store your payment information.

7. How We Use Data

We use collected data to:

  • Operate the Game and sync your progress
  • Serve and measure advertisements (if applicable)
  • Diagnose crashes and improve the Game
  • Provide customer support

We do not use your data to:

  • Sell personal information to third parties
  • Send marketing emails without consent
  • Build user profiles for purposes other than ad personalization

8. Data Sharing

We share data with the following third-party service providers:

  • Google LLC (AdMob): Advertising services — Privacy Policy
  • Supabase Inc: Authentication and database — Privacy Policy
  • Apple Inc (StoreKit): In-app purchases — Privacy Policy

We do not sell or rent your personal data to any other third parties.

9. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction (GDPR, CCPA, etc.), you may have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Opt out of personalized advertising
  • Request data portability
  • Withdraw consent for tracking at any time

To exercise these rights, contact us at raadbadr@gmail.com.

10. Children's Privacy

The Game is rated for ages 4+ and does not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13 without parental consent. Authentication requires an email address. If you believe a child has provided personal data without consent, contact us to request deletion.

11. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your data:

  • Encrypted HTTPS connections for all data transmission
  • Supabase Row Level Security (RLS) policies
  • No storage of payment credentials
  • Anon keys (not service keys) in the client app

However, no method of internet transmission is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.

12. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date. We recommend reviewing this page periodically.

13. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or your data, please contact us:

  • Email: raadbadr@gmail.com
  • Website: raadbadr.com
